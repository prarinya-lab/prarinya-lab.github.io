Home

Welcome to the Trustworthy Human-Centric Image Information Engineering Laboratory at Shinshu University. Our lab focuses on developing trustworthy and human-centric approaches to image processing, computer vision, and artificial intelligence. We strive to create innovative solutions that bridge the gap between advanced technology and human needs, ensuring reliability and transparency in our research outcomes.

Our mission is to advance the field of image information engineering while maintaining a strong focus on human-centric design principles and trustworthy AI systems. Through our research, we aim to contribute to society by developing technologies that are not only cutting-edge but also ethical and user-friendly.
