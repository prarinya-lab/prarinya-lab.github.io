Research Overview

Our laboratory focuses on developing trustworthy AI systems and advancing computer vision technologies. We work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and human-centric computing to create reliable and interpretable solutions that benefit society.

Key research areas include:

  • Trustworthy AI and Explainable AI
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Human-Centric Computing
  • Machine Learning Applications

Research Projects

Trustworthy AI and Explainable AI

Our research focuses on developing reliable and interpretable AI systems that users can trust. We work on methods to make AI decisions more transparent and understandable.

Key aspects:

  • Explainable AI methods for deep learning models
  • Bias detection and mitigation in AI systems
  • Robustness against adversarial attacks
  • Privacy-preserving machine learning

Computer Vision and Image Processing

Our computer vision research spans various applications and techniques, focusing on developing advanced algorithms for image understanding and analysis.

Key aspects:

  • Deep learning for image analysis
  • Object detection and recognition
  • Scene understanding and 3D reconstruction
  • Medical image analysis

Human-Centric Computing

We focus on developing AI systems that enhance human capabilities and improve human-computer interaction through intelligent interfaces and adaptive systems.

Key aspects:

  • Human-AI interaction design
  • Cognitive workload assessment
  • Adaptive interfaces
  • User behavior analysis
