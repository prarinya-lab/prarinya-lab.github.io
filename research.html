Research

Exploring the frontiers of AI and Computer Vision

Research Overview

Our laboratory focuses on developing trustworthy AI systems and advancing computer vision technologies. We work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and human-centric computing to create reliable and interpretable solutions that benefit society.

Key research areas include:

  • Trustworthy AI and Explainable AI
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Human-Centric Computing
  • Machine Learning Applications
